##  PROJECT 2: EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS (EDA) ON AN E-COMMERCE DATASET

## OVERVIEW
This project explores an e-commerce dataset to uncover meaningful patterns, trends, and insights. Using Microsoft Excel, I performed exploratory data analysis by examining key statistics, identifying data distributions, and analyzing customer and sales-related information. The goal was to transform raw data into actionable insights that can support business decisions and improve understanding of e-commerce performance.

## DATASET
The dataset used for this project is an e-commerce transaction dataset containing customer orders, product information, sales values, payment methods, and order-related details. The dataset was analyzed to understand customer purchasing patterns, sales performance, and operational trends.

*Key fields included:*
* Order ID
* Customer ID
* Product
* Quantity
* Unit Price
* Total Price
* Payment Method
* Order Status
* Referral Source
* Order Date

## KEY INSIGHTS
* The business generated a total revenue of **$1,264,761.96** from **1,200 orders**, with an average order value of **$1,053.97**.
* A total of **3,535 units** were sold, demonstrating strong customer demand across product categories.
* **Laptops** generated the highest revenue (**$192,126.56**), closely followed by **Printers** (**$195,612.61**) and **Chairs** (**$195,620.11**).
* **Instagram** was the top-performing referral source, generating the highest revenue (**$275,285.45**) and the largest number of orders (**259**).
* **Online payments** were the most frequently used payment method (**258 orders**), while **Credit Card** transactions generated the highest revenue (**$263,847.63**).
* Revenue peaked in **June ($170,616.13)**, indicating a period of exceptionally strong sales performance.
* The highest annual revenue was recorded in **2023 ($552,643.24)**, accounting for the largest share of total sales.
* Order statuses were relatively balanced, although **Cancelled (250)** and **Returned (247)** orders highlight opportunities to improve customer retention and fulfillment efficiency.


## RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Increase investment in high-performing marketing channels, particularly Instagram, as it generated the highest revenue and order volume.
2. Analyze the factors driving strong sales in June and replicate successful campaigns or promotions during lower-performing months.
3. Focus on promoting top-performing products such as Chairs, Printers, and Laptops through targeted marketing and inventory planning.
4. Investigate the causes of cancelled and returned orders to improve customer satisfaction and reduce revenue loss.
5. Continue optimizing online and credit card payment experiences, as these channels contribute significantly to transaction volume and revenue.


## CONCLUSION
The exploratory data analysis revealed valuable insights into sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and marketing effectiveness. The business generated over **$1.26 million** in revenue from **1,200 orders**, demonstrating strong overall performance. Instagram emerged as the most effective referral source, while Online and Credit Card payments played a significant role in revenue generation. Product demand was led by Chairs, Printers, and Laptops, and sales peaked during June and throughout 2023. Despite strong performance, the presence of cancelled and returned orders presents opportunities for operational improvement. Overall, the findings provide a solid foundation for data-driven decision-making and future business growth.
